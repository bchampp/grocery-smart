The world has an ongoing climate crisis, and being aware of your carbon footprint is becoming more relevant. For the typical student, being conscious of your carbon footprint is usually not a top priority. Being aware of where foods are coming from is a lot of work, and sourcing more environmentally friendly alternatives can be even more work. Although students would like to be more conscious of their footprint, the relevant information is not accessable enough to fit into a busy lifestyle. Grocery Smart's goal is to fill this void and provide users with a user friendly way to track their grocery habits in order to reduce waste and lower their carbon footprint.
