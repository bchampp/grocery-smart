About Us

Many university students struggle with the transition of living in residence to moving into their first student-house. One significant problem that often arises is in purchasing groceries. Far too often, students will purchase groceries with the intent of eating them, without understanding how long they stay good for. The result is food being thrown out weekly, without any easy tools to help improve this habit!

Grocery-Smart is an online platform that allows people to easily and effectively track their grocery consumption. It helps you track what groceries you have, visualize your food consumption and waste, and provide notifications when your food is going to go bad!
